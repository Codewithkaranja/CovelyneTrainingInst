About Covelyne

Covelyne Training Institute is a TVETA-approved beauty college offering professional courses in cosmetology, hairdressing, nail technology, and beauty therapy. We believe in hands-on learning that shapes confident, career-ready professionals.

Our mission is to provide high-quality, affordable, and hands-on training in beauty and cosmetology that empowers learners for entrepreneurship and employment.

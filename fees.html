About Us

We are a premier institution offering professional training in beauty therapy, cosmetology, hairdressing, and more. Covelyne Training Institute is a TVETA-approved institution. Our Main Campus is located at Shanbad House, Opposite New Naivas, Machakos Town, Kenya. We offer courses at competence and certificate levels. Part-time and evening classes are available.

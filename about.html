About Covelyne Institute

We are a premier institution offering professional training in beauty therapy, cosmetology, hairdressing, and more. Covelyne Training Institute is a TVETA-approved institution. Our Main Campus is located at Shanbad House, Opposite New Naivas, Machakos Town, Kenya.

We offer courses at competence and certificate levels with flexible learning options including part-time and evening classes to accommodate diverse student needs.
